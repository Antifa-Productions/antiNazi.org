Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

This website operates in accordance with the fundamental principle that minimal data collection preserves user autonomy and digital privacy. We do not track visitors, monetize personal information, or engage third-party analytics providers. Our commitment to privacy protection is integral to our mission of providing public-domain radical literature freely, without surveillance or data exploitation.

Key Practices at a Glance

No Tracking Technologies

We do not deploy cookies, pixel tags, device fingerprinting, or any other visitor tracking mechanisms.

No Analytics Services

Third-party analytics providers (including Google Analytics, Matomo, Plausible, and comparable services) are not utilized on this website.

IP Address Stripping

All IP addresses are removed from visitor request headers prior to logging. Consequently, IP addresses cannot be recorded or retained.

Referrer Header Stripping

HTTP Referer headers are stripped from all incoming requests. Referrer data cannot be captured or stored.

No User Authentication

This website does not require registration, authentication, or collection of any personally identifiable information.

Data Collection Practices

Automatically Generated Data

Upon visiting this website, web browsers transmit certain technical information to servers. Our system architecture ensures that identifying data is systematically removed:

  • IP Address — All IP addresses are stripped from visitor request headers prior to any logging operation. IP addresses are therefore never recorded.
  • HTTP Referer — Referer headers are stripped from all incoming requests. Referer data is not captured or stored.
  • User Agent String — Browser and device configuration information may be logged in access logs for operational purposes only.
  • Request Timestamp — Date and time of requests may be logged in access logs for operational purposes only.

All automatically generated access logs are retained for a maximum period of seven (7) days and are subsequently purged. Such data is never shared with third parties and is never utilized for user profiling or behavioral analysis.

Voluntarily Submitted Data

This website maintains no contact forms, comment submission systems, newsletter registration mechanisms, or any other infrastructure for collecting voluntarily submitted user data. Visitors have no means to transmit personal information to this website.

Cookies and Local Storage

This website does not utilize HTTP cookies. No cookie files will be transmitted to your browser from this domain.

In connection with Progressive Web Application (PWA) functionality (including offline accessibility via service worker), the following local storage mechanisms may be employed:

Service Worker Cache Storage
Content accessed during your browsing session may be cached locally within your browser for offline retrieval. This cache resides exclusively on your device and contains only publicly-available materials.
IndexedDB Storage
Where supported, cached content may be persisted in IndexedDB for performance optimization. Such data remains localized on your device and is accessible solely to you.

You retain the ability to clear this stored data at any time through your browser's configuration settings. Under no circumstances is this data transmitted back to our servers.

Third-Party Services and External Links

This website is intentionally architected to avoid dependence on third-party services for the following reasons:

  • Analytics Providers — No analytics services are employed; no statistical data is collected
  • Content Delivery Networks — Static assets are delivered directly from our own infrastructure
  • External Font Services — Typography relies exclusively on system fonts to eliminate external network requests
  • Social Media Integrations — No embedded buttons, activity feeds, or social sharing integrations are present
  • Advertising Networks — The website contains no advertising content and generates no advertising revenue

External hyperlinks to third-party websites (including Project Gutenberg, academic repositories, and related resources) are provided for informational reference only. We maintain no control over the privacy practices of such third-party sites and recommend independent review of their respective policies.

Security Safeguards

The following security measures are implemented to protect visitor data and ensure confidentiality:

  • Transport Layer Security — All communications are encrypted using TLS 1.3 with industry-standard cipher suites
  • Security Headers — Content Security Policy, X-Frame-Options, X-Content-Type-Options, and additional protective HTTP headers are enforced
  • System Maintenance — Server software and dependent libraries are maintained with current security patches
  • Administrative Access Controls — Administrative access to server infrastructure is restricted to authorized personnel only and access is logged

Legal Disclosure Obligations

We reserve the right to disclose log data where legally compelled by subpoena, court order, warrant, or other lawful judicial process. However:

  • Affected users will be notified of such disclosure requests to the extent legally permissible
  • We will contest overly broad, constitutionally problematic, or insufficiently particularized demands
  • Given our seven-day retention policy, substantially all requested data may have already been purged prior to receipt of any legal demand

User Privacy Rights

Due to the minimal nature of our data collection practices, your applicable privacy rights are correspondingly streamlined:

  • Right of Access — You may request copies of any logs containing identifiable information within the seven-day retention window
  • Right to Deletion — You may request immediate deletion of any retained data
  • Right to Object — You may object to any data processing activities (though processing is inherently minimal)
  • Right to Data Portability — You may receive any retained data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, please utilize the contact information provided below.

Children's Privacy

This website does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from minors. As no personal data collection mechanisms exist (including forms, authentication systems, or tracking technologies), minors may browse publicly-available literary materials without privacy concerns.

Policy Amendments

This privacy policy may be amended periodically. Any modifications will be:

  • Published on this page with an updated "last modified" date
  • Announced via the website's RSS feed for substantive changes
  • Non-retroactive — no version of this policy shall diminish privacy rights that existed under prior versions

Visitors are encouraged to review this policy periodically, particularly prior to utilizing any newly introduced website features.

Contact Information

PGP Encryption: Public keys available upon request for secure encrypted correspondence
Response Timeframe: All inquiries will be addressed within thirty (30) calendar days

All privacy-related requests shall be handled securely and treated with appropriate confidentiality.
